See it in action

Four views that transform how you understand your calendar.

Timeline View

Your killer feature. Browse your calendar organized by location, not just time. Watch your life unfold as location spans.

  • Location inheritance across days
  • Dynamic cover images per location
  • Time aggregation per location span

Map View

See your events on an interactive map. Understand where you spend your time with geographic clustering and rich popups.

  • Events clustered by location
  • Rich popups with activity breakdowns
  • Filter by day, month, or year

Availability Scheduler

Share your availability with a single link. Paint available time slots directly on the calendar.

  • Visual slot selection
  • Cal.com integration for bookings
  • Custom durations and buffer time
